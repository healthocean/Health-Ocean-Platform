# Requirements Document: Health Ocean

## Introduction

Health Ocean is a multi-sided marketplace platform that connects patients with diagnostic labs for home sample collection and online report delivery. The platform facilitates lab test bookings, phlebotomist dispatch, sample logistics, and digital report delivery across India's top 20 cities.

## Glossary

- **Patient**: End user who books lab tests through the platform
- **Lab_Partner**: Diagnostic laboratory that processes samples and generates reports
- **Phlebotomist**: Healthcare professional who collects blood/sample from patients at home
- **Booking**: A request for one or more lab tests with scheduled home collection
- **Test_Catalog**: Database of available lab tests with pricing and requirements
- **Sample**: Biological specimen collected from patient for testing
- **Report**: Digital test results generated by Lab_Partner
- **Collection_Hub**: Intermediate location for sample aggregation before lab delivery
- **LIS**: Laboratory Information System used by Lab_Partner
- **Dispatch_System**: Service that assigns and routes Phlebotomist to bookings
- **Admin**: Platform operator managing labs, catalog, and operations
- **Health_Package**: Bundled set of tests offered at discounted price
- **NABL**: National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Patient Registration and Authentication

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to register and securely access my account, so that I can book tests and view my health records.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a patient provides valid mobile number and OTP, THE System SHALL create a new account
2. WHEN a patient provides valid credentials, THE System SHALL authenticate and grant access
3. THE System SHALL store patient profile including name, age, gender, address, and medical history
4. WHEN a patient updates profile information, THE System SHALL validate and persist the changes within 2 seconds
5. THE System SHALL encrypt all patient data at rest and in transit

### Requirement 2: Test Search and Discovery

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to search and browse available lab tests, so that I can find the tests I need.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL provide a searchable catalog of at least 500 lab tests
2. WHEN a patient enters a search query, THE System SHALL return relevant tests within 1 second
3. THE System SHALL display test details including name, description, price range, preparation requirements, and sample type
4. THE System SHALL support filtering by test category, price range, and lab partner
5. THE System SHALL display health packages with bundled tests and discounted pricing
6. WHEN a patient views a test, THE System SHALL show available Lab_Partners with pricing and ratings

### Requirement 3: Booking Creation and Scheduling

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to book lab tests with home sample collection, so that I can get tested conveniently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a patient selects tests and confirms booking, THE System SHALL create a Booking with unique identifier
2. THE System SHALL allow patients to schedule collection within available time slots (6 AM to 9 PM)
3. WHEN creating a Booking, THE System SHALL assign a Lab_Partner based on availability, distance, price, and capacity
4. THE System SHALL collect patient address, preferred date, and time slot for sample collection
5. WHEN a Booking is created, THE System SHALL send confirmation to patient within 30 seconds
6. THE System SHALL support booking for multiple patients (family members) in a single transaction
7. WHEN a patient provides invalid address or unavailable time slot, THE System SHALL reject the booking and display error message

### Requirement 4: Payment Processing

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to pay for my booking securely, so that I can confirm my test appointment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support payment methods including UPI, credit card, debit card, and net banking
2. WHEN a patient initiates payment, THE System SHALL process it through a secure payment gateway
3. WHEN payment succeeds, THE System SHALL confirm the Booking and update status to "Confirmed"
4. WHEN payment fails, THE System SHALL mark Booking as "Payment Pending" and allow retry
5. THE System SHALL generate and send payment receipt to patient within 1 minute of successful payment
6. WHERE discount codes are applied, THE System SHALL validate and apply the discount before payment
7. THE System SHALL support partial refunds for cancelled bookings based on cancellation policy

### Requirement 5: Phlebotomist Dispatch and Assignment

**User Story:** As an admin, I want the system to automatically assign phlebotomists to bookings, so that sample collection is efficient.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Booking is confirmed, THE Dispatch_System SHALL assign an available Phlebotomist within 5 minutes
2. THE Dispatch_System SHALL consider phlebotomist location, availability, workload, and skills when assigning
3. WHEN no Phlebotomist is available, THE Dispatch_System SHALL notify Admin and mark Booking as "Pending Assignment"
4. THE Dispatch_System SHALL optimize routes for Phlebotomists with multiple bookings
5. WHEN a Phlebotomist is assigned, THE System SHALL send notification to both Phlebotomist and patient
6. THE System SHALL allow manual reassignment by Admin when automatic assignment fails

### Requirement 6: Sample Collection Workflow

**User Story:** As a phlebotomist, I want to manage my assigned bookings and collect samples, so that I can complete my work efficiently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Phlebotomist_App SHALL display a list of assigned bookings with patient details and navigation
2. WHEN a Phlebotomist arrives at patient location, THE Phlebotomist_App SHALL allow status update to "In Progress"
3. THE Phlebotomist_App SHALL support barcode scanning for sample identification and tracking
4. WHEN a sample is collected, THE Phlebotomist SHALL update status to "Sample Collected" with timestamp
5. THE System SHALL track Phlebotomist location in real-time during active bookings
6. WHEN a Phlebotomist cannot collect sample, THE Phlebotomist_App SHALL allow marking as "Failed" with reason
7. THE Phlebotomist_App SHALL provide turn-by-turn navigation to patient addresses

### Requirement 7: Sample Logistics and Tracking

**User Story:** As a lab partner, I want to track samples from collection to lab, so that I can ensure sample integrity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL track sample status through stages: Collected, In Transit to Hub, At Hub, In Transit to Lab, Received at Lab
2. WHEN a sample reaches Collection_Hub, THE System SHALL update status within 5 minutes
3. THE System SHALL maintain chain of custody records for all samples with timestamps and handler information
4. WHEN a sample is delayed beyond expected time, THE System SHALL alert Admin and Lab_Partner
5. THE System SHALL support barcode scanning at each logistics checkpoint
6. THE System SHALL calculate and display estimated lab arrival time based on current location

### Requirement 8: Lab Partner Integration and Report Generation

**User Story:** As a lab partner, I want to receive sample information and upload reports, so that I can complete the testing workflow.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a sample arrives at lab, THE System SHALL notify Lab_Partner with booking and sample details
2. THE Lab_Partner_Dashboard SHALL allow Lab_Partner to accept or reject sample based on quality
3. THE System SHALL integrate with Lab_Partner LIS using HL7 or FHIR standards
4. WHEN Lab_Partner uploads a report, THE System SHALL validate format and associate with correct Booking
5. THE System SHALL support report formats including PDF and structured data
6. WHEN a report is uploaded, THE System SHALL notify patient within 5 minutes
7. THE System SHALL enforce turnaround time tracking (12-48 hours from sample receipt)

### Requirement 9: Report Delivery and Access

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to view and download my test reports, so that I can access my health information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a report is available, THE System SHALL send notification via app, email, and SMS
2. THE Patient_App SHALL display all reports with test name, date, and download option
3. THE System SHALL allow patients to view reports in-app and download as PDF
4. THE System SHALL maintain report history for at least 5 years
5. THE System SHALL support sharing reports with doctors via secure link
6. WHEN a patient requests a report, THE System SHALL retrieve and display it within 2 seconds
7. THE System SHALL redact sensitive information when sharing reports externally

### Requirement 10: Real-time Tracking and Notifications

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to track my booking status in real-time, so that I know when to expect the phlebotomist.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Patient_App SHALL display current booking status with estimated arrival time
2. WHEN Phlebotomist is en route, THE Patient_App SHALL show live location on map
3. THE System SHALL send push notifications for status changes: Assigned, En Route, Arrived, Completed
4. THE System SHALL send SMS notifications for critical updates when app is not active
5. WHEN estimated arrival time changes by more than 15 minutes, THE System SHALL notify patient
6. THE Patient_App SHALL allow patients to contact Phlebotomist via in-app call

### Requirement 11: Lab Partner Onboarding and Management

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to onboard and manage lab partners, so that I can expand the platform's service coverage.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support lab partner registration with NABL certification verification
2. THE System SHALL validate lab partner credentials including licenses and certifications
3. WHEN a lab partner is approved, THE System SHALL create Lab_Partner account with dashboard access
4. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow configuration of lab capacity, service areas, and operating hours
5. THE System SHALL support enabling or disabling lab partners based on performance
6. THE Admin_Panel SHALL display lab partner analytics including bookings, ratings, and turnaround times

### Requirement 12: Test Catalog Management

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to manage the test catalog, so that I can keep test information accurate and up-to-date.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow adding new tests with name, description, category, and requirements
2. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support bulk upload of tests via CSV or Excel
3. WHEN a test is updated, THE System SHALL reflect changes across all platforms within 5 minutes
4. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow setting base prices and lab-specific pricing
5. THE System SHALL support test variants (e.g., fasting vs non-fasting glucose)
6. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow marking tests as active or inactive

### Requirement 13: Pricing and Discount Management

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to manage pricing and discounts, so that I can run promotions and optimize revenue.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow setting platform commission rates (15-40%) per lab or test
2. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support creating discount codes with validity period and usage limits
3. WHEN a discount code is applied, THE System SHALL validate eligibility and calculate final price
4. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow creating health packages with bundled tests and package pricing
5. THE System SHALL support dynamic pricing based on demand, time, and location
6. THE Admin_Panel SHALL display pricing analytics and revenue reports

### Requirement 14: Booking Cancellation and Refunds

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to cancel my booking if needed, so that I can get a refund according to the cancellation policy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Patient_App SHALL allow cancellation of bookings before sample collection
2. WHEN a patient cancels more than 4 hours before scheduled time, THE System SHALL process full refund
3. WHEN a patient cancels less than 4 hours before scheduled time, THE System SHALL process 50% refund
4. WHEN sample is already collected, THE System SHALL not allow cancellation
5. THE System SHALL process refunds within 5-7 business days
6. WHEN a booking is cancelled, THE System SHALL notify Lab_Partner and Phlebotomist immediately

### Requirement 15: Rating and Review System

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to rate my experience, so that I can provide feedback and help other users.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a booking is completed, THE System SHALL prompt patient to rate the experience
2. THE System SHALL collect ratings (1-5 stars) for Phlebotomist, Lab_Partner, and overall experience
3. THE System SHALL allow patients to write text reviews with ratings
4. THE System SHALL display average ratings for Lab_Partners and Phlebotomists
5. WHEN a rating is below 3 stars, THE System SHALL flag for Admin review
6. THE System SHALL prevent duplicate ratings for the same booking

### Requirement 16: Health History and Records

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to maintain my health history, so that I can track my health over time.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Patient_App SHALL display chronological health history with all test results
2. THE System SHALL support uploading external reports not from the platform
3. THE Patient_App SHALL visualize trends for common parameters (e.g., blood sugar, cholesterol)
4. THE System SHALL allow patients to add notes and tags to reports
5. THE Patient_App SHALL support filtering health records by date range, test type, or parameter
6. THE System SHALL export complete health history as PDF

### Requirement 17: Compliance and Data Privacy

**User Story:** As a patient, I want my health data to be secure and private, so that my information is protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL comply with Indian medical record storage regulations
2. THE System SHALL encrypt all patient health data using AES-256 encryption
3. THE System SHALL implement role-based access control for all user types
4. THE System SHALL maintain audit logs for all data access and modifications
5. THE System SHALL allow patients to delete their account and associated data
6. THE System SHALL obtain explicit consent before sharing data with third parties
7. WHEN a data breach is detected, THE System SHALL notify affected users within 72 hours

### Requirement 18: Multi-language Support

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to use the app in my preferred language, so that I can understand the content easily.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support at least 5 Indian languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi)
2. WHEN a patient selects a language, THE System SHALL display all content in that language
3. THE System SHALL translate test names, descriptions, and UI elements
4. THE System SHALL maintain English as fallback for untranslated content
5. THE System SHALL detect device language and suggest appropriate language on first launch

### Requirement 19: Analytics and Reporting

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to view platform analytics, so that I can make data-driven decisions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Admin_Panel SHALL display real-time dashboard with key metrics: bookings, revenue, active users
2. THE Admin_Panel SHALL provide reports on booking trends, popular tests, and geographic distribution
3. THE Admin_Panel SHALL show lab partner performance metrics including turnaround time and ratings
4. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support exporting reports as CSV or PDF
5. THE Admin_Panel SHALL display financial reports including commissions and payouts
6. THE System SHALL track and display conversion funnel from search to booking completion

### Requirement 20: Dispute Resolution

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to manage disputes between patients and lab partners, so that I can ensure fair resolution.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL allow patients to raise disputes for bookings with issues
2. THE Admin_Panel SHALL display all open disputes with details and timeline
3. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow Admin to communicate with patient and Lab_Partner
4. WHEN a dispute is resolved, THE System SHALL update status and notify all parties
5. THE System SHALL support issuing refunds or credits as dispute resolution
6. THE System SHALL maintain dispute history for audit purposes

### Requirement 21: Phlebotomist Management

**User Story:** As an admin, I want to onboard and manage phlebotomists, so that I can ensure quality sample collection.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support phlebotomist registration with credential verification
2. THE System SHALL validate phlebotomist certifications and licenses
3. THE Admin_Panel SHALL allow setting phlebotomist service areas and availability
4. THE System SHALL track phlebotomist performance including completion rate and ratings
5. THE Admin_Panel SHALL support enabling or disabling phlebotomist accounts
6. THE System SHALL maintain phlebotomist training records and certification expiry dates

### Requirement 22: Capacity Management

**User Story:** As a lab partner, I want to manage my testing capacity, so that I don't receive more bookings than I can handle.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Lab_Partner_Dashboard SHALL allow setting daily testing capacity per test type
2. WHEN capacity is reached, THE System SHALL stop assigning new bookings to that Lab_Partner
3. THE Lab_Partner_Dashboard SHALL display current utilization and remaining capacity
4. THE System SHALL allow Lab_Partner to temporarily pause accepting bookings
5. THE Lab_Partner_Dashboard SHALL support setting capacity for future dates
6. WHEN capacity is low, THE System SHALL alert Lab_Partner to increase capacity

### Requirement 23: Emergency and Critical Results

**User Story:** As a lab partner, I want to flag critical test results, so that patients can be alerted immediately.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a test result is critically abnormal, THE Lab_Partner SHALL flag it as critical
2. WHEN a critical result is uploaded, THE System SHALL immediately notify patient via call, SMS, and app
3. THE System SHALL display critical results with prominent visual indicators
4. THE System SHALL recommend immediate medical consultation for critical results
5. THE System SHALL maintain a log of all critical result notifications
6. WHEN a critical result is flagged, THE System SHALL notify Admin for follow-up

### Requirement 24: Subscription and Membership Plans

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to subscribe to membership plans, so that I can get discounts on regular tests.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL offer subscription plans with monthly or annual billing
2. WHERE a patient has active subscription, THE System SHALL apply automatic discounts on bookings
3. THE System SHALL provide subscription benefits including free home collection and priority scheduling
4. WHEN a subscription expires, THE System SHALL notify patient 7 days in advance
5. THE System SHALL support auto-renewal of subscriptions with saved payment methods
6. THE Patient_App SHALL display subscription status and benefits

### Requirement 25: Responsive Web and Mobile Experience

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to access the platform on any device, so that I can book tests conveniently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL provide responsive web application accessible on desktop and mobile browsers
2. THE System SHALL provide native mobile apps for Android and iOS
3. WHEN accessed on mobile browser, THE Web_App SHALL adapt layout for smaller screens
4. THE System SHALL maintain feature parity between web and mobile apps
5. THE System SHALL synchronize data across devices in real-time
6. THE Mobile_App SHALL support offline viewing of downloaded reports
7. WHEN network is unavailable, THE Mobile_App SHALL queue actions and sync when connected
